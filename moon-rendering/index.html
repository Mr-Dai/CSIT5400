• Use mouse, ctrl and shift to rotate, pan and zoom the moon.
  • Press l key to switch between headlight and distant light source.
  • Press spacebar to toggle the animation of the distant light source.
  • Press different num key to view different stage:
    • Stage 0: A greyish sphere
    • Stage 1: Texture mapping
    • Stage 2: Normal mapping
    • Stage 3: Face mapping
